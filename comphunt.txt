Component Hunt

Youtube is one of the popular websites and its components are broken down in the following hierarchy :

App Shell (The Master Container)
- Navbar (Search bar, Voice search, User settings)
- Sidebar (Collapsed or Expanded navigation links)
- Main Content Area
- - Video Player Container (The actual player + controls)
- - Video Info Section (Title, Views, Date)
- - Channel Action Bar (Avatar, Subscriber count, Join/Sub buttons)
- - Comment Section (Sort button, Add comment, Comment list)
- - Recommendation Sidebar (List of "Up Next" video cards)



Structure :

Root (App)
 ├── Navbar (Header)
 │    └── SearchGroup
 │         ├── Input
 │         └── Button
 ├── LayoutContainer (Main)
 │    ├── VideoContent (Left Column)
 │    │    ├── Player
 │    │    └── MetaData
 │    └── Recommendations (Right Column)
 │         ├── VideoCard
 │         └── VideoCard (Repeated)
 └── Sidebar (Nav)
